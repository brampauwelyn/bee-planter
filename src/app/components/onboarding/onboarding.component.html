Every year the beepopulation is decreasing due to various reasons (climate change, use of pesticides, etc.)
You can help and protect the bees by planting beefriendly flowers and trees.

BeePlanter helps you choose beefriendly plants for your garden!

Wanne see how it works?

Filter plants by color

BeePlanter makes it possible to search for plants by color, so you can choose plants of you're favorite color.
Btw, Did you know that bees don't see colors the same as humans?

Filter plants by time of flowering

Just like humans bees need food throughout the whole year.
If you want a beefriendly garden, it is good idea to search for plants which are flowering during winter time.
